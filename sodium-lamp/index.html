Model boundary: what is solved vs. assumed

Solved on GPU

Axisymmetric advection–diffusion of heat, fuel, oxidizer, and active neutral sodium; a stiff two-level Na 3s↔3p rate balance; and a cylindrical P1 photon-diffusion solve with wall/end escape.

Deliberate non-LTE rule

The atomic population is never assigned from a Boltzmann distribution. LTE is calculated on a separate path and displayed only as a falsifiable reference.

Inputs needing experiment

Salt-to-neutral-Na activation, chemical excitation, quenching, effective line redistribution, wall reflectivity, and the imposed recirculating flow/flame-holder field. These are exposed rather than tuned invisibly.

Not yet predictive

No detailed Na/Cl/O/H kinetics, Navier–Stokes pressure solve, conjugate ceramic heat transfer, multi-line redistribution, PV electrical model, or validated Lightcell dimensions.

Reduced model geometry follows the public concentric-cylinder combustor and annular return-flow concept. Physics constants are embedded and inspectable; no server or model API is involved.
